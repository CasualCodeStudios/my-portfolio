About Me

Coding & Creativity

I’m a frontend website developer with a strong mastery of HTML and CSS. Coding is more than just work for me — it’s my source of entertainment, even when it gets stressful. I enjoy the rhythm of solving problems, the satisfaction of clean code, and the clicky voice of a mechanical keyboard when I’m typing at high speed. My passion lies in creating responsive, user‑friendly websites that blend functionality with design, much like art on a digital canvas.

Outside of coding, I find joy in art and drama. Sitcoms like The Big Bang Theory make me laugh, while live stage performances captivate me with their raw human energy. These passions keep me balanced, inspired, and energized, fueling both my creativity and discipline as a developer.
